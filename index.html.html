What the data tells us

Emerging Leaders has reached over 100,000 people globally since 2014. Kenya remains the heart of the programme — consistently accounting for the majority of reach and providing strong infrastructure for global expansion. The 2020 dip reflects COVID-19 disruption. The 2022–2023 rebound shows strong recovery momentum, and the RTF Pilot is opening new frontiers in Colombia, South Africa and India.
